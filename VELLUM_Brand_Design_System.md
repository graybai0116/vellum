# VELLUM — Brand Design System

## Positioning

VELLUM is a **visual intelligence platform** that translates aesthetics into structured prompt language. It feels like a creative intelligence system and premium design tool for creators — not a generic AI tool or prompt generator.

> Every image contains a hidden visual syntax — lighting, composition, texture, emotion, rhythm. VELLUM extracts that language and transforms it into reusable creative prompts.

**Core slogan:** Translate aesthetics into prompts.
**Alternatives:** See style. Extract language. / Visual language, extracted. / Aesthetic intelligence.

**Name origin:** Vellum = refined archival paper used for manuscripts. Suggests craftsmanship, precision, creative archives, timeless visual culture.

---

## Brand Personality

Intelligent · Curated · Editorial · Minimal · Precise · Artistic · Quiet Luxury

**Inspired by:** Aesop, Linear, Framer, Apple, Notion
**Not:** typical AI startups, cyberpunk interfaces, neon-heavy "future tech"

| Product | Perception |
|---|---|
| Midjourney | Chaotic artist |
| Runway | AI startup |
| Adobe | Enterprise software |
| **VELLUM** | **Aesthetic intelligence** |

---

## Logo

**Direction A — Editorial V:** Ultra-thin geometric "V" inspired by editorial typography, crop marks, camera framing. Monochrome, thin strokes, wide spacing.

**Direction B — Framed Glyph:** "V" integrated into an aperture/framing icon. Represents visual extraction, structured aesthetics.
